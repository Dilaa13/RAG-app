Company Policy on Remote Work

Purpose:
This policy outlines the guidelines for employees who work remotely, either full-time or part-time.

Eligibility:
All full-time employees who have completed at least three months of service are eligible to request remote work arrangements. Approval is based on performance, job function, and department needs.

Expectations:
Employees must be available during standard working hours (9 a.m. to 5 p.m.) and remain responsive on official communication channels such as email and Microsoft Teams. Work output, deadlines, and quality must be consistent with on-site performance standards.

Data Security:
Company data must only be accessed through approved VPN connections. Use of personal devices for confidential work requires prior IT approval.

This policy is reviewed annually and may be updated as business or technological needs change.
